Brief Bio

    I received my PhD from the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University in 2008. My thesis advisor was William W. Cohen.

    I have worked as a scientist and/or engineering manager in R&D intitutions such as Snapchat Research, Microsoft Research, Microsoft Bing, Qualcomm Research, Inome and Ericsson R&D.

    I have multiple patents, conference papers, and 10+ years of R&D experience in Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Search, Analytics, Data Mining, Information Retrieval and Information Extraction.

    I live in San Diego, CA.
